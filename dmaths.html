In double maths, my class does the maths A-level this year and further maths next year. That means we have 6 papers to do in May and June just for maths. 4 of these papers are pure mathematics, just numbers and how they work without any practical application (for example logarithms, circles), and the other 2 papers are mechanics and statistics. (for example frictoin, motion and probability).
